Fabio Pizzati

I am a postdoctoral researcher at MBZUAI, working with Ivan Laptev. Previously, I was a postdoc at the Torr Vision Group at the University of Oxford, working with Phil Torr. I am also a member of ELLIS.

I did my PhD at Inria Astra-vision team, in Paris (France), where I was supervised by Raoul de Charette.

I am currently interested in large-scale video diffusion models. In particular, I care about video generation for robotics and embodied AI applications, extending to world modeling and physics-aware learning. I am also interested in AI safety and multi-agent LLM systems.
