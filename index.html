Fabio Pizzati

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Torr Vision Group at the University of Oxford, working with Phil Torr.

Previously, I was a co-tutelle PhD at Vislab/Ambarella, located in Parma (Italy) and Inria Astra-vision team, in Paris (France), where I was supervised by Raoul de Charette.

I am currently working on large scale diffusion models for image generation, and continual learning. During my PhD, my research focused on generative adversarial network models, in particular for modeling adverse weather and low-light scenarios.
